# Final Analysis

## Zero-shot
- Strength: strict schema improved structure
- Weakness: model sometimes adds extra explanation

## Few-shot
- Strength: better boundary learning
- Weakness: still struggles with ambiguous tickets

## Chain-of-thought
- Strength: improved numerical reasoning
- Weakness: occasional overconfidence in assumptions

## LLM-as-judge
- Strength: consistent rubric-based scoring
- Weakness: bias toward polite responses

## Self-consistency
- Strength: reduces variance in outputs
- Weakness: expensive multiple calls required

## Tree-of-thought
- Strength: good trade-off analysis
- Weakness: sometimes over-scores complexity

## Rephrase-and-respond
- Strength: reduces ambiguity effectively
- Weakness: depends heavily on first rephrase quality
